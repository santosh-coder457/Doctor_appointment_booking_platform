Surgeries

Appendectomy

Surgical removal of the appendix.

Cholecystectomy

Surgical removal of the gallbladder.

Hernia Repair

Surgical repair of a hernia.

Knee Replacement

Surgical replacement of a knee joint.

Thyroidectomy

Partial or complete removal of the thyroid gland, often for thyroid nodules or cancer.

Cesarean Section (C-section)

Surgical delivery of a baby through abdominal and uterine incisions.

Hysterectomy

Removal of the uterus.

Hip Replacement

Replacing a damaged hip joint with a prosthesis.

Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)

Creating new pathways for blood flow around blocked heart arteries.

Cataract Surgery

Replacement of a clouded eye lens with an artificial one.
